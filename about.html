About

iTranslate is an image recognition application that can both recognise what is in an image and translate that into a language you want to learn


Users can use their phone camera and the app can translate seamlessly. There is also the option to upload photos from your device or social media accounts.


The larger database of images we have, the faster, more reliable your searches are! Of course your images are private and safe with us. However, if you do choose to publically share your photos to our large community on this service, you can too! Users can save images to their public profile. Lastly, instead of uploading their own images to learn, users can search and browse other publically saved images too!
